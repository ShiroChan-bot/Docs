### How To Use
- **Tips:**
  - _Split argument message using_ `|`
  - _Use_ `true`/`false`
  - _Options_ `Length`**|**`Numbers`**|**`Symbols`**|**`Lowercase`**|**`Uppercase`
    - Length*
    - Numbers*
    - Symbols*
    - Lowercase*
    - Uppercase*
- **Example:**
  - /passwordgenerator 20|true|false|true|true
